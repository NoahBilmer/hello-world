My Story

I begain programming on and off sometime in early 2020, ever since then, my passion for technology has only grown. In high school I was introduced to Visual Basic, which was where I learned many of the fundementals of programming.

After high school I took a gap year to try to figure out what I'd like to study in college. I decided to challenge myself to the limit by enrolling in CS50, and I genuinely think it's one of the best decision I've ever made, it sparked a love for programming in me that has not slowed down in the slightest.

Here at Algonquin College im continuing to challenge myself and learn a wide range of programming languages and technologies. Some of my favourite programming langauges to work with include...

  • C - I like how the language challenges us to really understand how algorithmns are built, with little hand holding.
  • Java - I appreciate how explicit Java is, it forces us to think about the structure of our programs.
  • Python - I like Python because it allows us to focus on the bigger picture of a program, and flesh out the details using its many read-made tools.
